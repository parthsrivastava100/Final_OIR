About Us

The IIT Kanpur community is driven by the resolve to explore, incubate and implement ideas to further the cause of academia, research and innovation. The globally acclaimed academic curriculum at IIT Kanpur prepares students for fascinating careers all over the world and cultivates a critical thinking led approach while imparting knowledge in science and technology, engineering, management and humanities. Researchers at IIT Kanpur do cutting edge research in their arena. There is great synergy between the fundamental research and applied research done at IITK. The atmosphere of IITK tries to inculcate the spirit of curiosity and the excitement of discovery. Our alumni and faculty have achieved international laurels and they strive for more. We pursue, encourage and facilitate strong, cordial and long term collaborative and exchange programmes. These involve participation in technology development, research and education and they mutually benefit the faculties and students of both, ours and our partner Universities.

Experience India

We at IITK, celebrate a myriad of diversity brought in by students and faculty belonging to various regions of India. Students and Faculty celebrate India’s traditions and cultures at their own level here.

Life@IITK

Our beautiful campus boasts of greenery, sustainability, striving for social change and celebrates the contemporary-traditional IITK culture.

Cutting edge Research

The diversity and depth of research projects undertaken at IITK by professors and students alike is an exceptional measure of the innovation and discovery track record of research at IITK

Academic Flexibility

ITEC Programs

IIT Kanpur organized two ITEC programs starting from 17 February 2020 – Introduction to Data Science (2 weeks) and Leadership in the Modern Business Context (1 week). The courses were taught by IITK faculty and were attended by participants from countries like Ethiopia, Bangladesh, Zambia, Malawi, Sri Lanka, Oman, Russia and Afghanistan.

We will also be setting up the IITK-Rice Collaborative Research Center which will impart training to students and researchers and conduct research in areas related to clean and sustainable energy technologies in the early stage of its inception, with possibility of expansion into various other areas of not only science and engineering but also humanities.

Introduction to Cyber Security

A two-week-long program on "Introduction to Cyber Security", sponsored by the Indian Technical and Economic Cooperation Programme (ITEC) of the Ministry of External Affairs, Government of India, was conducted at IIT Kanpur.A new batch of trainees from different nations including Tanzania, Ghana, Kenya, Vietnam, Bhutan, and several other countries, started the basic level Cyber Security Course at C3I Center, IITK under the guidance of Sandeep Shukla (Head, CSE)

Asian Smart Cities Research Project

La Trobe University Australia launched Asian Smart Cities Research and Innovation Network today at Australian High Commission. IIT Kanpur is part of the initiative and will collaborate with La Trobe on various research projects in Smart Cities domain. Vice Chancellor of La Trobe Prof John Dewar was present.This will help boost the opportunities for the students as well as for the nation.
