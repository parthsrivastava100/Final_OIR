Academics has been of utmost importance to faculties and students at IIT Kanpur. This tradition has led IIT Kanpur’s academics to be one of the best in India as well as be recognized at the global level.
We offer the following programs:

  • ✔ BTech program
    – Students are selected through Joint Entrance Exam (Advanced).
  • ✔ BS program
    – Students are selected through Joint Entrance Exam (Advanced).
  • ✔ BTech-MTech dual degree program
    – Students in their BTech program can opt for this program on the basis of their CPI.
  • ✔ MTech Program
    – Students are selected through Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering (GATE).
  • ✔ MDes Program
    – Students are selected through both GATE and Common Entrance Exam for Design (CEED).
  • ✔ MSc Program
    – Students are selected through Joint Admission Test for MSc (JAM).
  • ✔ MBA Program
    – Students are selected on the basis of Common Admission Test (CAT).

The Academic System:

The Academic system at IIT Kanpur is very flexible. On top of the conventional programs offered, there are various ways one could pursue any other field of their interest. Some additional programs offered are:

  • Large number of Other Electives (OEs): Students in their BTech program get a lot of opportunity to take up courses of other departments in their pre-final and final years.
  • Minors: The various departments at IIT Kanpur offer a plethora of minors for the students to choose from. The minors are allotted on the basis of one’s CPI. Apart from the conventional minors, at IIT Kanpur, you could do a minor in Industrial and Management Engineering (IME) and Cognitive Sciences as well!
  • Dual degree program – Students are not offered 5-year BTech MTech programs right after JEE Advanced at IIT Kanpur as we believe that before attending the BTech program, one does not know if they would want to pursue a master’s in it. So, this program is offered to students who decide to do the master’s degree while they attend their BTech degree at IIT Kanpur.
  • Double Major program – This is a unique initiative taken up at IIT Kanpur, where if a student is diligent enough to pursue another major from IIT Kanpur, they could opt for this program! This extends you degree by a year and the second major too is mentioned on your degree. So, you could be graduating with a BTech in Computer Science and Engineering with a Double Major in Economics!
