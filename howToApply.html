How To Apply

Planning to join 'the Indian' Institute of Technology Kanpur (IITK)?
We welcome you to the IITK community and help you to achieve your academic goals in a friendly and professional environment. IITK gives you the excellent opportunity of academic as well as cultural exposure.

Admission Form for Non-Degree Students

Foreign nationals can either apply for Degree Programs (Master/Doctoral) or Non-Degree Programs (Course work / Research not leading to a degree) here. Admission into IIT Kanpur's undergraduate programs is conducted by the Joint Admission Board, all of which is elaborated in the section on Programs offered. In addition, IIT Kanpur offers a flexible academic curriculum in terms of course template, open electives, majors, minors, with the atmosphere of ingenuity and true learning. IIT Kanpur provides the unique opportunity to students to apply at any time of the year and these students will be invited to campus at a time of mutual convenience and understanding.
