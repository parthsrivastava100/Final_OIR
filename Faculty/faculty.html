Faculty

IIT Kanpur maintains a rich and thriving culture for research owing to the state-of-the-art infrastructure and facilities provided to faculty as well as students. We generate thousands of research opportunities and have had international research collaborations as well. IIT Kanpur invites overseas universities’ faculties to pursue research here and also encourages our own faculty members to pursue research opportunities abroad too. Our faculties have a massive number of projects to their name and have tons of research papers and publications under their wing and have achieved numerous awards for their momentous research. IIT Kanpur was also among the first IIT’s to establish the Department of Research and Development which has several cells under it to look after the interest of research scholars in the institution. Annually the number of publications, patents, and projects has seen an upward scale and IIT Kanpur aims to keep this evergreen research culture’s spirit ever high with the support of its faculty and students.
